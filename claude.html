About Literium

Founded in 2015, Literium has revolutionized the way readers discover and enjoy digital books. Our mission is to make world-class literature accessible to everyone, everywhere.

We believe that every book has the power to transform lives. That's why we've built a platform that celebrates authors, supports publishers, and delights readers with an unparalleled digital reading experience.

With over 100,000 titles and partnerships with 500+ publishers worldwide, we're proud to be your gateway to literary excellence.
